Our Story

Main Mountain was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality landscaping and excavation services with integrity, reliability, and exceptional customer service. As a veteran-owned and operated business, we bring the same discipline, attention to detail, and commitment to excellence that we learned in military service to every project we undertake.

Based in Cameron Park, California, we serve residential and commercial clients throughout the area with a comprehensive range of services including trash hauling, tractor work, landscaping, wood chipping, light excavation, and tree stump removal.

What sets us apart is our dedication to customer satisfaction and our commitment to doing the job right the first time. We take pride in transforming properties and solving problems for our clients.
